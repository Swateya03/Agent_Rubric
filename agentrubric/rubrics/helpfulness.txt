# Helpfulness rubric — evaluates how helpful a response is

CRITERIA:
1. Directness (weight: 0.35)
   Does the response give a direct, actionable answer without unnecessary padding?

2. Practicality (weight: 0.35)
   Are the suggestions or information practically usable in the real world?

3. Depth (weight: 0.30)
   Does the response go beyond surface-level to provide genuinely useful detail?

SCORING:
Each criterion is scored 0.0 to 1.0.
Final score = weighted sum of all criteria scores.
